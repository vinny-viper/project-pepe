Bulk Rename Utility

A complete guide to batch renaming files and folders.

Mastering Bulk Renaming

Why Choose Bulk Rename Utility?

  • **Batch Renaming:** Rename hundreds or thousands of files at once.
  • **Incredibly Powerful:** Offers over a dozen renaming methods, including adding dates, removing characters, and adding numbering.
  • **Live Preview:** See exactly what your new file names will look like before you apply the changes.
Download Bulk Rename Utility

A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Installation

Download and install Bulk Rename Utility from its official website. The installer is simple and you can accept the default settings. It will integrate with your right-click context menu automatically.

Step 2: Select Your Files

Open Bulk Rename Utility. Navigate to the folder containing the files you want to rename using the folder tree on the left. Select the files you want to rename from the list in the middle pane.

Step 3: Define Renaming Rules

Use the different sections in the top pane to set your renaming rules. You can add prefixes, suffixes, replace characters, and even add sequential numbers. The bottom pane will show you a live preview of the new filenames, so you can make sure everything is perfect before you commit.

Step 4: Rename

Once you are happy with the live preview, click the "Rename" button in the bottom-right corner. A confirmation window will appear. Click "OK" to apply the changes to all your selected files.
