Revo Uninstaller

A complete guide to removing stubborn programs.

A Guide to Clean Uninstalls

Revo Uninstaller is a powerful tool that helps you remove programs completely, including leftover files and registry entries.

Download Revo Uninstaller

You can download the program from the official website. Download Here

Step 1: Install Revo Uninstaller

Download Revo Uninstaller from its official website. The free version is powerful enough for most tasks. Once the download is complete, run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts to set it up on your PC. The installation is quick and straightforward, and you can choose to enable the program's Hunter Mode for quick uninstalls.

Step 2: Uninstall the Program

Open Revo Uninstaller. You will see a list of all the programs installed on your computer. Find the program you wish to uninstall, right-click on it, and select "Uninstall." Revo will first run the program's built-in uninstaller. Be sure to follow the prompts of the original uninstaller before moving to the next step.

Step 3: Scan for Leftovers

After the standard uninstaller finishes, Revo will give you three scanning modes: Safe, Moderate, and Advanced. We recommend choosing the **Advanced** scan. This performs a thorough search for any leftover files, folders, and registry entries that the program left behind. Registry entries are essentially a database of settings and options for your Windows operating system. Deleting a program with leftover registry entries can cause system instability, so a clean sweep is crucial!

Step 4: Remove Leftover Items

Revo will present you with a list of all the leftover items it found, categorized by registry entries and files/folders. Review the list (you can double-check if you're unsure), click "Select All" and then "Delete" to perform a clean uninstall. Your computer is now free of any remnants of the old program!
